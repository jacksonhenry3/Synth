The earth is warming at an alarming rate. It's important to understand this and appreciate the depth of this issue. This audio chart uses surface temperature anomaly data from the NOAA. The higher the pitch of the audio the warmer the planet. This data is the yearly deviation from the 20th century average in degrees Celsius from 1880 to 2013. The temperature deviation data is then linearly mapped to an A major scale spreading over 8 octaves. The tone switches every 300ms representing the data for the next year.
